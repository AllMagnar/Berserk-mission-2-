About Berserk (short)

Berserk,by Kentaro Miura, is an epic dark fantasy exploring fate, free will, ambition and the cost of revenge. The story centers on Guts, Griffith, and the Band of the Hawk, concluding in cruel betrayals and the Eclipse.

Iconic Characters

  • Guts — The Black Swordsman
  • Griffith — The Ambitious Hawk
  • Casca — Warrior And Survivor
